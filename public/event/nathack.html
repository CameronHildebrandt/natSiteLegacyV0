How to Register

1.  Register as a participant on both Devpost and through NeurAlbertaTech's internal sign-up form. The individual registration deadline to receive exclusive natHACKS merch is July 5 at 23:59 MDT. The final registration deadline is July 15 at 23:59 MDT.


2.  Apply for brain-computer interface hardware to use during the event (if desired). The hardware application deadline is July 9 at 23:59 MDT.


3.  Form a team (if desired). If you (like many others) are looking for teammates, we have a channel dedicated to that on Discord! Teams should be declared through NeurAlbertaTech's team declaration form no later than July 30 at 17:00 MDT.


That's all you need to do to get registered!
Next, be sure to join our Opening Ceremony on July 30th at 18:00 MDT and get ready to start hacking right after at 19:00 MDT! Lastly, MAKE SURE to submit your project on Devpost before August 2nd at 10:00 MDT.

natHACKS

Registration Opening Soon
natHACKS is an event like no other, inspiring beginners to develop practical neurotech skills and challenging competent hackers to apply themselves in this growing and exciting field. Spanning two weeks and culminating in a 64-hour hackathon weekend, the event will combine workshops, challenges, and networking opportunities for anyone interested in neurotechnology. With three different streams and separate judging criteria based on experience level, we're thrilled to allow neurotech enthusiasts to get their hands dirty in a diverse selection of projects.

Begins: July 29th, 2022
Ends: August 1st, 2022

Info Sessions

You can join one of our info sessions, held through June and July for more details on natHACKS before signing up. You'll be able to see brain interface hardware in action and be able to engage with other members of the Alberta neurotech community!
